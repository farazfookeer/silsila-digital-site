01 / About

20+ years turning ambiguity
into working systems

Silsila is a solo consultancy led by Faraz Fookeer — Senior BA and Product Owner with two decades across buy-side and sell-side financial institutions, specialising in front-office systems, collateral management, fixed income, and repo trading technology. Currently completing an MA in AI Ethics & Society at Birkbeck, University of London.

The name Silsila (سلسلة) means chain or unbroken transmission in Arabic — most powerfully used in Sufi tradition to describe the lineage from teacher to student. It reflects how I work: connecting business intent, technical reality, and human adoption into a single, accountable chain.

05 / Philosophy

How I work

Clarity over cleverness
The best solution is the one people actually understand and use. I optimise for adoption, not elegance in isolation.
AI fluency, not AI first
I understand what AI can and cannot do. I bring that fluency to every engagement without assuming AI is the answer to every problem.
Honest evaluation
No vendor partnerships. No commissions. No incentive to recommend anything other than what actually works for your situation.
Adab
An Arabic concept meaning proper conduct and respect. I treat every engagement — every person, every system, every constraint — with the seriousness it deserves.
